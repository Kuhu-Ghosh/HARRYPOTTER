HARRY POTTER AND THE GOBLET OF FIRE

Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire is the fourth book in J.K. Rowling’s series. In this story, Harry is unexpectedly entered into the dangerous Triwizard Tournament, a magical competition between three wizarding schools. Despite being underage and not submitting his own name, Harry is forced to compete. As he faces three challenging tasks, he uncovers a plot involving Barty Crouch Jr., who impersonates a teacher using Polyjuice Potion and manipulates the tournament. The final task leads Harry to a trap where Lord Voldemort regains his full body and powers. Harry narrowly escapes with the help of Cedric Diggory’s body and a magical portkey. The book marks a darker turn in the series as Voldemort returns to power, and the wizarding world begins to face the threat of his rise once again.
