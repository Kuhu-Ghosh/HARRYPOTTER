HARRY POTTER AND THE ORDER OF THE PHOENIX

Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix is the fifth book in the series by J.K. Rowling. In this story, Harry returns to Hogwarts after a difficult summer, only to find that most people refuse to believe that Lord Voldemort has returned. The Ministry of Magic appoints Dolores Umbridge as the new Defense Against the Dark Arts teacher, and her strict control over the school leads Harry and his friends to form a secret group called Dumbledore’s Army to learn real defensive magic. Harry also struggles with disturbing visions linked to Voldemort’s mind. Eventually, he has a vision of Sirius Black being tortured, which leads him and his friends to the Department of Mysteries. There, they face Death Eaters and learn more about a prophecy that connects Harry and Voldemort. The battle ends with the tragic death of Sirius and the official confirmation that Voldemort has returned, setting the stage for the war to come.
