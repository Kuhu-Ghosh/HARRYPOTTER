HARRY POTTER AND THE PRISONER OF AZKABAN

Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban is the third book in J.K. Rowling’s series. In this installment, Harry learns that a dangerous prisoner named Sirius Black has escaped from Azkaban and is believed to be hunting him. As Harry returns to Hogwarts, the school is guarded by terrifying creatures called Dementors.While uncovering the truth behind Black’s escape, Harry discovers that Sirius is actually his godfather and was falsely accused of betraying his parents. The real traitor is Peter Pettigrew, who has been hiding in plain sight as Ron’s pet rat. With help from Hermione’s Time-Turner, Harry and Hermione go back in time to save Sirius and a magical creature named Buckbeak. Though Sirius cannot clear his name yet, he escapes, and Harry finally learns more about his parents' past and those who truly cared for him.
